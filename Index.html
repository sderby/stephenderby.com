In an industry awash in sameness, it’s time for us to stand out. To harness what makes us great, and use it to propel us forward. Because we’re dedicated to doing what others can’t. 

 

Motivated to say yes when others say no. 

 

Defying what is expected and delivering more than they could ever imagine.

While other companies fine-tune how they work from day-to-day, Suffolk is the inquisitive pioneer for those seeking more. It’s what makes us different: built on our construction heritage, but designed to expand into other less expected dimensions of our industry. 

Driven by our culture. Shaped by the values and spirit of our team. Our guiding principles drive everything we do. They build on our core values—passion, integrity, hard work, and professionalism—and make us stand apart from others in the industry; uniting us no matter our geography, role, or function. 

 

Our purpose is to redefine possible.
